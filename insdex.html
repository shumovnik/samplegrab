// features

Pro tools.
Browser native.

🎚️
Gain Control

Dial in the perfect input level with a precision rotary knob. Boost quiet streams or tame loud sources — from −18 dB all the way up to +12 dB.

🛡️
Brick-wall Limiter

Never clip a recording again. The built-in limiter with configurable attack and release times protects your recordings from sudden loud transients.

📊
Real-time VU Meter

Professional stereo level meter with peak hold and clip indicator. Monitor both L and R channels independently while you record.

〰️
Live Waveform

Watch your recording build in real time on the scrolling waveform canvas. Annotated with time markers for instant orientation.

⏮️
Retro-Rec Buffer

Always-on background buffer captures the last 30s, 60s, or 5 minutes — even before you hit record. Never lose that perfect moment again.

✂️
Waveform Editor

Trim, select, loop and normalize your clips directly inside the extension. No need to open a DAW for quick edits.

📦
Library & ZIP Export

All your clips stay in a built-in library. Export individual files or bundle everything into a single ZIP archive — one click.

⚙️
Smart Settings

Auto-normalize on stop, auto-download, Save As dialog, custom download subfolder — tune the workflow exactly to how you work.

🆓
Free to Use

Core recording features are completely free. No account needed, no watermarks, no time limits on recordings.

// how it works

Three steps
to studio quality.

01
Install the extension

Add SampleGrab to Chrome in one click from the Web Store. No sign-up, no setup wizard, no nonsense.

02
Open any tab & hit record

Navigate to YouTube, Spotify, a podcast, a video call — anything. Click the record button and SampleGrab starts capturing the tab's audio.

03
Edit & export

Trim, normalize, and download as MP3 or WAV. Your clips live in the built-in library until you're ready to export.
